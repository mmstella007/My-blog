About Me

Hi, I'm Monica

By day, I'm a Digital Product Owner — someone who lives in sprint plans, stakeholder calls, and an embarrassing number of Notion tabs. But when the laptop finally closes, I become something else: a curious, quietly restless person chasing good food, slow mornings, honest conversations, and the kind of travel that rearranges your perspective a little.

This little space on the internet is not a portfolio. It's not a brand. It's just… me. The version of me that still gets excited about a new recipe, cries at good books, overthinks everything, and genuinely believes a home-cooked meal can fix a hard week.

I started writing here to make sense of things — because writing is how I understand my own life. If something here resonates with you, even quietly, then this corner of the internet has done exactly what I hoped.
